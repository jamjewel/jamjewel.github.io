The Zeroo app

The Zeroo app employs a machine learning model to predict user purchase patterns, offer tailored product recommendations, and minimize food wastage. Users input product details and expiry dates, receiving reminders and the option to donate to nearby NGOs. Donations trigger notifications, aiding NGO inventory planning, while users receive tokens for discounts on future purchases.
